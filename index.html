Game systems

Designed around control, luck, builds, and items.

Auto-aim attacks keep the focus on movement and survival decisions, while weapons, chips, map items, and dice rolls push each run into a different shape.

Stage Structure Goal-based survival stages instead of endless-only play, with 6-15 minute sessions built for repeat runs.
Movement Depth 3D maps, Z-axis routes, jump, dash, and aerial escape options add strategy beyond flat survival movement.
Growth Variety Weapon growth, enhancement chips, passive items, map rewards, and dice outcomes create large build gaps.
Streamer Moments Jackpot rolls, risky builds, time attacks, and clutch boss clears create moments that are easy to clip and share.
