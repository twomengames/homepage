## Main Game

### LEGENDARY LUCK DICE / Spawner Hunter

**Genre:** 3D Roguelike Action Survival  
**Platform:** PC Steam  
**Engine:** Unreal Engine 5  
**Tone:** Dark fantasy, stylish action, cinematic survival  
**Studio:** TwoMenGames  
**Location:** Seoul, Korea

Spawner Hunter is a 3D roguelike action survival game where players fight massive monster waves and hunt powerful Spawners.

The game is positioned between Vampire Survivors, Risk of Rain 2, and Returnal, expanding familiar survival growth into a 3D action-focused format.

---

## Key Features

### Massive Monster Battles

Fight escalating waves of monsters in pressure-heavy survival combat.

### 3D Bullet-Hell Action

Use Z-axis movement, jump, dash, aerial movement, and positioning to survive situations that flat survival games cannot create.

### Dice-Powered Progression

Luck rolls can amplify growth, open comeback chances, and create dramatic jackpot moments.

### Boss Hunting

Grow efficiently, locate randomized Spawner bosses, and clear each stage before time runs out.

### Roguelike Replayability

Fast loops move from level-up to choice, deckbuilding, growth, boss clear, and repeat. Sessions are designed around roughly 6-15 minute runs.

### Stylized Dark Fantasy World

A moody fantasy atmosphere with a clean modern UI direction built for PC players.

---

## Game Systems

- Goal-based stage clear structure instead of endless-only survival
- Auto-aim attacks so players can focus on movement, evasion, and positioning
- Weapon growth, enhancement chips, passive items, map rewards, and dice outcomes
- Strong build gaps through rarity-based growth choices
- Time attack, ranking, and difficulty-based challenge structure
- Streamer-friendly jackpot rolls, risky builds, and clutch boss clears
